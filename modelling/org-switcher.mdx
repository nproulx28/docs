***

**Step 1:** Click the Organization dropdown found in the top right corner of your interface. This icon is found between the question mark icon for help center and the scenario dropdown.

<Frame caption="The organization dropdown is displaying the currently selected company: 'Acme Corp.'">
  <img src="/images/modelling/zPonVpIweC1ctgVRWZhY.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

This display will inform you of your current organizations. All other organizations you belong to will appear in a vertical dropdown once you have clicked on the current organization dropdown triangle. (pictured above)

**Step 2:** Click the dropdown triangle beside your current organization. 

\
**Step 3:** Click on the new organization you wish to view.

<Note>
For more information on the organization settings, please reference the ‘Organization Settings’ section of this guide.
</Note>
