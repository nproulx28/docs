Dashboards allow you to keep track of certain drivers, metrics, and accounts in a more user-friendly and visual way than you are typically used to. Each dashboard is unique to the user, and depending on your permissions - only those above you in your organization hierarchy will be able to see this data. 

Let’s take a look at some of the features!
