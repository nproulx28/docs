<Note>
Multi-entity is only available on **Pluvo Advanced** and **Pluvo Enterprise** Plans.  Reach out to your account manager or [support@pluvo.io](mailto:support@pluvo.io) for more info. 
</Note>

If you haven't done so yet, [connect your first accounting integration](/modelling/features/integrations/accounting-integrations).

Once you've connected your accounting system, you may repeat the same steps to connect any additional entities you may have. 

<Note>
By default, your organization currency will be the company currency of the first accounting system you connect. You can change this later in your organization settings.
</Note>

## Filter Accounts by Entity 

Once you have multiple accounts connected, a dropdown will become available on account pages allowing you to filter which entities' accounts are visible in the grid. 

![](/images/modelling/YidaDrUDR8j3IdG21z1r.png)

<Note>
When multiple entities are visible at once, the auto-generated bar charts are hidden from teh account view. This is to reduce confusion when displaying accounts in currencies on a single chart. 

Use [Dashboards & Charts](/modelling/features/dashboards-and-charts) to combine accounts in different currencies on charts.
</Note>

## Report on Multiple Entities

When you add an additional accounting integration, Pluvo  will auto-generate another set of financial statements. under the reporting tab.

To learn how to manage or edit these reports, visit [Reports](/modelling/archive-keep-hidden/archived-pages/reports) or [Report templates: The 3-Statement Model](/modelling/archive-keep-hidden/archived-pages/reports/report-templates-the-3-statement-model)

Accounts from different entities can be easily combined on reports or charts, and may be referenced in any formula in metrics, drivers, or forecasts.
