## Xero Integration

Pluvo integrates with **Xero** to automatically pull in your financial data, so your models stay accurate and up to date without manual entry.

### What We Sync from Xero

Once connected, Pluvo imports:

* **Chart of Accounts**\
  All active GL accounts from your Xero org, including account names, codes, and types.
* **Account Balances**\
  Monthly actuals for P\&L and Balance Sheet accounts—used to populate actuals in your models.
* **Account Metadata**\
  Categories like Income, Expense, Asset, and Liability are preserved to support rollups and filtering.

### How Syncing Works

* Data is synced **manually** by default via the **Sync** button in the top right
* You can sync as often as needed to pull in the latest actuals
* **Auto-sync** can be enabled if you prefer continuous updates

### Notes

* If you modify your chart of accounts in Xero, new or updated accounts will appear in Pluvo after the next sync\
  **You must manually update** any variables that rely on those accounts
* Pluvo is a **read-only integration**—we never push data back to Xero
