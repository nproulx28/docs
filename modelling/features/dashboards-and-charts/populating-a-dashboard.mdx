title: "Populating a Dashboard"
description: "\"
---

***

**Step 1:** Click on ‘Dashboard’ from the navigation bar. <br />

**Step 2:** Ensure that you have your desired scenario selected. 

\
**Step 3:** Click on the dashboards dropdown, and then ‘New Dashboard”

**Step 4:** Give your new dashboard a name and click ‘Save’.<br />

**Step 5:** Click on the (+) symbol within one of the empty widgets. 

\
**Step 6:** Assign a title, chart type, and select your variable.

<Tip>
Your new graph will populate the dashboard. Repeat these steps for as many metrics/drivers as necessary!
</Tip>
