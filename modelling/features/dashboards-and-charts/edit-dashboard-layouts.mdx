Customizing your dashboard layouts are a great way to personalize your experience. 

To enter the dashboard layout editor, click the `Edit Layout` button in the top right of the dashboard page. 

<Note>
Dashboard layouts are unique to each dashboard. Changing a layout on one dashboard will not affect your other dashboards - however, the changes will be applied to that dashboard across all your scenarios. 
</Note>

![](/images/modelling/FbqfSbesxohZ11ELvk4z.png)

## Resize Charts 

Once you are in the layout editor, you can resize charts by clicking and dragging on the bottom right or bottom left corner of a chart. When a chart is resized, Pluvo will do its best to fit the remaining charts on screen. You may need to resize or reorder other charts to make a layout that fits nicely within your aspect ratio. Chart sizes snap to equal increments to ensure a compact grid layout. 

## Reorder Charts

To reorder charts, simply click on the handle in the top left of the chart and drag them into the desired order.
