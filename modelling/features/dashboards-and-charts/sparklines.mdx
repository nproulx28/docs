Sparklines are small charts that display in grids to help you quickly visualize how an amount is changing over time.

Sparklines always display dynamic amounts - meaning they will show the `actual` amount for past data (denoted by a solid line), and the `forecast` amount for future dates. 

![](/modelling/files/lwyF0Nvxni9PfpUORsrX)

The date range that sparklines display is always the same as the associated grid.

The dot indicates the current date.
