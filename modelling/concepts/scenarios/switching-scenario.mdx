**Step 1:** Click on the scenario dropdown in the top right corner of your screen. You will find this menu beside the organization dropdown discussed previously.

<Frame caption="<p>In this case, 'Budget 2024-2025' is the current scenario.</p>">
  <img src="/files/l17xwnSfMmtEDRLJr4B8" alt="" />
</Frame>

The dropdown menu that greets you will display your active scenario. A list of all other created scenarios will appear vertically below the ‘New Scenario’ button. 

**Step 2:** Select your desired scenario from the list of existing scenarios. 

To create a new scenario, select the New Scenario button and choose an existing scenario to clone.
