***

**Step 1:** Navigate to the scenarios drop down in the top right corner of your screen.

In order to create a new scenario, you will need to click on the scenario dropdown menu. In the previous section, we simply clicked between existing scenarios. To create a new one you will need to press the ‘New Scenario’ button.  

**Step 2:** Click on ‘New Scenario’, select an existing scenario to cline, and then give your new scenario a name. 

**To Edit or Delete a scenario** – click the three dots next to your current scenario in the scenario dropdown.
