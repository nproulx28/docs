Within this tab, you will be able to: 

1. Rename your organization 
2. Permanently delete your organization.

<Warning>
**Warning!** Deleting your organization is irreversible. This action will permanently delete all your data and you will lose access to Pluvo. 

Contact [support@pluvo.io](mailto:support@pluvo.io) if you wish to proceed. 
</Warning>
