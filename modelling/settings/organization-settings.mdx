***

The organization settings hub will allow you to:

* Manage your organization details; 
* Manage your user permissions;
* Configure your forecasts;
* Manage your integrations, and; 
* Pull up a detailed audit log of all changes made to your organization. 

To access your organization settings, click on the Organization selection dropdown menu found at the top right corner of your interface.
