<Update label="April 7, 2026">
  ## v1.16.0

  Pluvo version 1.16.0 drops in the next couple of days. Highlights below.

  ### Refreshed settings pages

  All settings pages have been updated to match Pluvo's current look and feel. Functionality is unchanged — just much shinier. You can preview them at [dev.pluvo.io](https://dev.pluvo.io/) under **org settings** or **account settings**.

  ### Improved user permissions

  - **New `Guest` role.** Limited to view and comment actions, no editing. Designed for department heads, board members, and others who need visibility into the business in Pluvo but shouldn't be editing data.
  - **Base scenario merges restricted to admins.** Previously, anyone with edit access to the base scenario could merge into it.
  - **Members now have edit access to the base scenario by default.** Previously, members only had view access, which caused confusion when onboarding colleagues.
</Update>
